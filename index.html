The Space

Sunlight, soft linen, a quiet corner.

The studio sits on the second floor of a historic chapel with unique mediterranean charm in Northwest Portland. Brick and tile, arched entry, a palm tree at the door. Inside: tall windows, warm wood floors, a comfy sofa to settle into, and a small tiled fireplace that makes the space feel more like a home than a clinic.

It's the kind of room that asks you to exhale the moment you walk in.

Your Therapist

Emma Martin, LMT

Emma's practice is grounded in a simple promise: to create a space where you feel genuinely welcomed, and a treatment shaped to what your body actually needs that day, not a one-size routine.

Whether you're here for quiet unwinding or targeted relief, expect thoughtful pressure, intuitive work, and an environment designed for comfort. Clients often describe sessions as grounding, rejuvenating, and restorative.

A small sanctuary in the middle of the city. You're in good hands from the moment you step inside.
